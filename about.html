About us

Joining Wolverton Town is easy, just come to the clubhouse in Osborne Street most evenings of the week and then fill in a form so we know hwo you are and how to contact you. Alternatively Junior training night is Friday from 6:00pm any time from the first Friday in May We are running senior nets at Radcliffe School old hall every Thursday from 6:30pm. for juniors and seniors at Leon School 13;00 on Sunday until April We are seeking players of all abilities to support Three Saturday and One Sunday Team

Our vision is to be the best club in the area

Our mission is to promote organise and foster amateur cricket of the highest standard in the Wolverton area. Wolverton Town CC encourages community participation and membership is open to all. Central to our ethos is enjoyment of the game and the development of young cricketers and elite players
